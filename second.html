FasTEST® Analytical Technology

FasTEST analytical technology provides rapid and accurate analysis of aquatic herbicide and algaecide concentrations in water. FasTEST data are used for monitoring treatment programs and regulatory compliance.

FasTEST analytical methods have been developed and verified for monitoring ten active ingredients, including the following SePRO aquatic products:  

ProcellaCOR®, Sonar®, Renovate®, Renovate® MAX G, Sculpin® G, Galleon®, Clearcast®, Nautique®, Komeen®, Captain®, Captain® XTR, K-Tea®, Habitat® and Stingray®.
